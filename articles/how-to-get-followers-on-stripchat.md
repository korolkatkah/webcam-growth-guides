# How to Get Followers on Stripchat

Followers matter differently than raw viewers: a high follower-to-viewer ratio is itself a ranking signal (see [Stripchat Algorithm](stripchat-algorithm)), and followers are the audience most likely to come back and tip consistently.

## Relying Only on Stripchat Doesn't Scale Followers Fast

Stripchat's internal discovery can bring you viewers, but converting strangers into followers happens mostly through repeated exposure — which is easier to engineer off-platform than by waiting for the same person to stumble into your room twice.

## The Multi-Platform Approach

Different platforms bring followers in different ways, and using more than one tends to outperform betting on a single channel:

- **TikTok** — reaches cold audiences at scale; good for broad top-of-funnel awareness even if conversion per view is low.
- **Reddit** — smaller reach, but drives highly qualified traffic from niche-relevant communities who convert to followers at a higher rate.
- **Twitter/X** — best for converting an audience you already have, since followers there opted in and respond well to broadcast notifications when you go live.

Rather than relying solely on Stripchat's internal discovery algorithm, treat these as an audience-building layer that fills your broadcasts before they even start.

## Practical Follower-Building Tactics

1. Post consistently on at least one outside platform — irregular posting undermines the habit-forming effect that turns viewers into followers.
2. Give people a reason to follow *before* they're already watching — teaser content, schedule announcements, behind-the-scenes posts.
3. Make your streaming schedule easy to find everywhere your content lives, so a new fan can actually act on interest.
4. Use "going live" notifications on platforms that support them (Twitter/X in particular) — this converts existing followers into same-day viewers.
5. Treat your bio/link page as a hub, not an afterthought — it's often the only thing standing between a new viewer and actually following you.

## Followers Feed Back Into Ranking

A stronger follower base doesn't just mean more return viewers — the follower-to-viewer ratio itself strengthens your standing in Stripchat's ranking algorithm, creating a compounding effect over time.
