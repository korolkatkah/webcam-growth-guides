# Chaturbate Hashtags: How to Choose and Rotate Them

Chaturbate has no official category system — hashtags in your room status and title are how the discovery engine actually works. Getting this right matters more than on most platforms.

## What Hashtags Actually Do

Adding hashtags to your broadcast puts your room into Chaturbate's tag search engine, so anyone searching for a specific look, act, or fetish sees your room alongside others using the same tag. The most popular tags are also surfaced directly on the homepage, above the general chat listing.

## Types of Tags to Use

- **Descriptive tags** — #blonde, #teen, #bigboobs — describing who you are.
- **Origin/location tags** — #latina, #europe — useful for audience targeting.
- **Show-type tags** — #squirt, #anal — describing what's actually happening.
- **Toy tags** — #lovense, #ohmibod — signaling interactive-toy availability.

## Rotation Is the Real Strategy

Tags aren't a set-and-forget setting. Some models report a **20–30% increase in viewership** simply from rotating hashtags regularly rather than leaving the same set indefinitely — competition shifts as other rooms adopt the same tags, and a fresh combination can surface you to a different slice of searchers.

## Don't Overdo It

Piling on dozens of tags at once tends to annoy viewers and dilutes your positioning rather than helping. A focused, accurate set that actually matches your content works better than casting the widest possible net.

## A Practical Approach

1. Pick 4–6 tags that honestly describe your content and audience.
2. Include at least one toy tag if you use interactive toys — see [Lovense Tips for Chaturbate](lovense-tips-for-chaturbate).
3. Rotate a portion of your tag set every week or two rather than keeping it static.
4. Watch which combinations actually correlate with more viewers over time, and lean into those.
