# Chaturbate Private Show Tips: Pricing and Strategy

Private shows are one of the highest per-minute revenue streams on Chaturbate, but pricing and setup decisions strongly affect how often public-room viewers actually convert into a private session.

## Typical Pricing

- **Private shows** commonly run **30–90 tokens per minute**, model-set, with a typical 10-minute private totaling **$24–$33**. Most models lock in a **5- or 10-minute minimum** rather than allowing pay-by-the-second entry.
- **Spy shows** let another viewer watch an ongoing private without participating, typically **6–25 tokens per minute** — a lower-cost, lower-engagement option layered on top of your private rate.
- Tokens cost viewers **$0.08–$0.11 each** depending on package size, and Chaturbate keeps roughly 50% as a platform fee — factor that into how you think about your effective per-minute rate.

## Getting Viewers Into Private Shows

1. **Tease clearly in the public room.** Give viewers enough sense of what a private show includes that the decision to go private feels like an easy upgrade, not a leap of faith.
2. **Set a minimum-time policy and stick to it.** A consistent 5- or 10-minute minimum sets clear expectations and protects your time from very short, low-value privates.
3. **Use spy shows as a bridge.** A viewer unsure about committing to a full private can convert through the cheaper spy option first, then upgrade once invested.
4. **Keep private availability visible** — mention it in your bio and periodically in chat, the same way you'd repeat a [tip menu](chaturbate-tip-menu-ideas).

## Private Shows vs. Public Tipping

Public-room income depends on volume and menu structure (see [Chaturbate Tip Menu Ideas](chaturbate-tip-menu-ideas)). Private shows convert a smaller number of highly interested viewers into guaranteed per-minute income — the two are complementary, not competing, revenue streams.
