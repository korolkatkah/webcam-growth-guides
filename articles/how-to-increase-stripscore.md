# How to Increase StripScore

If you already know [how StripScore works](how-stripscore-works), this is the checklist version — concrete actions in rough priority order.

## 1. Stabilize Your Hourly Earnings

Since tokens-per-hour is the heaviest-weighted input, the highest-leverage move is smoothing out your income per session rather than chasing one big spike. A structured, three-tier tip menu (small/mid/premium actions) keeps tipping steady throughout a session instead of relying on rare large tips.

## 2. Complete Your Profile Fully

- Add at least one photo album.
- Add at least one video.
- Fill out your bio completely, including your tip menu and schedule.

This is a fixed, one-time task with a measurable payoff — do it before anything else on this list if you haven't already.

## 3. Fix Technical Reliability

- Test your connection stability before going live, not during.
- Confirm your microphone is working and audio levels are consistent.
- Avoid streaming on networks known to drop or throttle (public Wi-Fi, overloaded home networks during peak hours).

## 4. Increase Content Variety

Rotate show formats instead of running the same routine every session — goals, games, themed segments, and toy-interactive shows all count as variety signals, and they also happen to keep human viewers around longer.

## 5. Stay Within Platform Guidelines

Review Stripchat's model guidelines periodically — they get updated, and accounts that drift from current recommended practices tend to lose ranking ground over time even without a specific violation.

## 6. Be Patient With New Accounts

If your account is new, expect a calibration period of roughly 4–8 weeks before these changes fully reflect in your ranking. Many models change tactics too early and never see the payoff of what they were already doing right.

## Combine With External Traffic

StripScore optimization improves your position in Stripchat's *own* model list. It doesn't add outside viewers. Pairing it with external traffic — such as [EWOhub's Stripchat traffic promotion](https://ewohub.com/stripchat-promo) — compounds both effects instead of relying on internal ranking alone.
