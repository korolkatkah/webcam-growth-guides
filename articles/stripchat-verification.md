# Stripchat Verification: Requirements and Common Mistakes

Verification is the first gate every new model has to clear, and most delays come from a small set of avoidable mistakes rather than genuine eligibility problems.

## What You Need

- **A valid government-issued ID**: passport, driver's license, or national ID card.
- **Clear photos** of the document — no glare, blur, or shadows.
- **A selfie holding the ID**, with both your face and the document clearly visible.
- **Personal information matching the ID exactly**, including name spelling.
- **Confirmation you're 18 or older**, verifiable from the selfie and ID together.

## Accepted Documents

- **Passport** — generally the smoothest option since it shows identity clearly in one document.
- **National ID card** — front and back both required.
- **Driver's license** — front and back both required.
- **State ID card** — front and back both required.

Student IDs, work badges, and other non-government documents are not accepted.

## Processing Time

Verification typically takes **24–72 hours**. Submitting incomplete or low-quality documents is the most common reason this stretches longer.

## Common Rejection Reasons and Fixes

- **Glare, blur, or shadows on the ID photo** → retake in natural daylight without flash, directly facing the camera.
- **Name entered differently than shown on the ID** → copy the spelling exactly as printed, including middle names or initials if present.
- **Hair or glasses obscuring the face in the selfie** → pull hair back and remove glasses if lighting causes glare.
- **Selfie makes you appear younger than 18** → retake with clearer lighting and a straight-on angle showing your full face.
- **Non-government ID submitted** → resubmit with a passport, driver's license, or national ID specifically.

## Regional Age Verification

Some US states (including Louisiana, Texas, and Florida) have additional age-verification requirements that Stripchat complies with, which may add an extra ID-verification step depending on where you're accessing the platform from.
