# Stripchat Promotion: Every Type of Traffic Strategy Explained

"Stripchat promotion" covers several genuinely different strategies that get lumped together. Knowing which type you're actually doing (or missing) makes it much easier to diagnose slow growth.

## 1. Internal (On-Platform) Promotion

This is everything Stripchat's own systems control: your [StripScore](how-stripscore-works), the broader [ranking algorithm](stripchat-algorithm), tag selection, and platform features like the [new model boost](stripchat-new-model-boost). It's free but bounded by the platform's existing traffic. Full breakdown: [Stripchat Internal Promotion](stripchat-internal-promotion).

## 2. Organic External Promotion

Building an audience on social platforms (TikTok, Reddit, Twitter/X), search-visible content, or a personal page, then funneling that audience into your room. It's slower to build but isn't rented — the audience is yours. See [How to Get Followers on Stripchat](how-to-get-followers-on-stripchat) and [Stripchat SEO / External Traffic](stripchat-seo-external-traffic).

## 3. Paid / Managed Traffic Promotion

Dedicated services that drive additional real viewers into your room from outside the platform, layered on top of whatever your organic and internal standing already is. This is what [EWOhub's Stripchat traffic promotion](https://ewohub.com/stripchat-promo) does — it's designed to compound with, not replace, ranking and content work.

## 4. Affiliate-Driven Traffic

Traffic sent by webmasters and affiliates through Stripchat's official affiliate network (Stripcash). This mostly matters for studios and models working with agencies rather than solo performers — more in [Stripchat Affiliate Traffic](stripchat-affiliate-traffic).

## Which One Should You Focus On?

- **New account, no following:** start with internal promotion basics (profile, tags, schedule) — they're free and immediate.
- **Established room, plateaued:** organic external promotion usually unlocks the next tier of growth.
- **Need faster results now:** paid promotion adds volume without waiting for organic channels to mature.
- **Working with a studio or agency:** affiliate and managed traffic likely already factor into your numbers — worth understanding even if you don't run it yourself.

Most models who grow steadily use at least two of these four at once rather than betting on a single channel.
