# How to Increase Chaturbate Earnings

Chaturbate keeps roughly **50% of tokens tipped** as a platform fee — a 200-token tip (about $20) nets the model around $10. Given that split, converting the audience you already have matters as much as attracting new viewers.

## Understand Where Tokens Actually Come From

- **Public room tipping**, driven by a well-structured [tip menu](chaturbate-tip-menu-ideas).
- **Private shows**, typically **30–90 tokens per minute**, with most 10-minute privates running **$24–$33** total and often locked to a 5- or 10-minute minimum.
- **Spy shows**, a cheaper voyeuristic option at roughly **6–25 tokens per minute**, letting undecided viewers watch without participating. See [Chaturbate Private Show Tips](chaturbate-private-show-tips).
- **Tip-triggered interactive toys**, which measurably increase engagement and tip volume. See [Lovense Tips for Chaturbate](lovense-tips-for-chaturbate).

## What Actually Increases Earnings

1. **Streaming your real peak hours**, not just the platform-wide average — see [Best Time to Stream on Chaturbate](best-time-to-stream-on-chaturbate). Models who find their personal peak windows report 40–80% higher earnings per hour.
2. **Structuring a tiered tip menu** with a low-cost entry point (1–5 tokens) to lower the barrier for new tippers, plus mid and premium tiers for regulars.
3. **Using tip goal automation** to keep momentum going across a session rather than relying on random, unprompted tips.
4. **Offering private and spy shows** as a next step for engaged viewers rather than leaving all revenue to public-room tipping.
5. **Building a stable, returning audience** through consistent scheduling — repeat tippers are worth more over time than one-off new viewers.

## Diversify Beyond Live Tips

Bots, tip goals, and toy integration (see [Best Chaturbate Bots/Apps](best-chaturbate-bots-apps)) help maximize what you earn *while* live, but income streams outside live hours — social content, off-platform promotion, and consistent branding — reduce how dependent your earnings are on any single session.
