# Stripchat Internal Promotion Explained

Internal promotion is everything Stripchat itself controls to decide which rooms get visibility. Understanding these mechanics matters as much as anything you do off-platform.

## New Model Boost

New accounts get a temporary visibility boost — historically around a **15-day window** from your first stream, with the platform having extended new-model promotion to run closer to a **full month** in recent updates. During this period, being active as much as possible (ideally close to round-the-clock in the early days) maximizes what the boost actually delivers. Full detail: [Stripchat New Model Boost](stripchat-new-model-boost).

## Contests

Stripchat runs recurring model contests, with categories split out so newer or smaller-following models aren't just competing directly against the platform's top earners. Placing well in a contest is itself a visibility event, independent of your regular StripScore.

## StripScore-Driven List Placement

Outside of boosts and contests, your baseline position in the model list is governed by [StripScore](how-stripscore-works) — hourly earnings consistency, profile completeness, stream reliability, and content variety.

## Tag and Category Placement

Stripchat's discovery surfaces (tag pages, category browsing) are also internal promotion — your visibility here depends on tag selection and competition level, covered in [Stripchat Hashtags / Tags](stripchat-hashtags-tags).

## Making the Most of Internal Promotion

1. If you're new, treat the boost window as a limited-time opportunity, not just a nice bonus — the activity you put in during those first days/weeks compounds.
2. Enter contests where your category fit is realistic rather than chasing the biggest one.
3. Don't let StripScore basics slide once your boost period ends — that's when the account calibration period (4–8 weeks) really starts to matter.
4. Revisit tags periodically since competition shifts over time.

Internal promotion is free but capped by Stripchat's existing traffic pool. Pairing it with [external traffic promotion](https://ewohub.com/stripchat-promo) extends growth past what internal mechanics alone can deliver.
