# Stripchat Hashtags / Tags: How to Choose Them

Stripchat doesn't publish an official technical spec for how tags affect discovery, but the practical pattern is consistent and well-documented across model guides: tag competition level matters more than tag popularity.

## Why the Biggest Tags Usually Hurt You

The most obvious, highest-traffic tags also carry the most rooms competing for the same eyeballs. Being one of several thousand rooms under a mega-tag means your realistic chance of surfacing to a browsing viewer is low, no matter how good your show is.

## The Moderate-Competition Sweet Spot

Tags used by roughly **500–2,000 rooms** tend to convert better than the biggest tags — enough search volume to matter, low enough competition that a well-optimized room can actually surface. This is the single most actionable tagging principle available.

## A Practical Tag Selection Process

1. List tags that honestly describe your content, niche, and appearance — don't tag for reach you can't back up in the show itself.
2. Check how many rooms currently use each candidate tag.
3. Favor tags in the moderate range over the largest ones, especially early on.
4. Keep one or two broader tags for baseline reach, but don't rely on them as your primary discovery path.
5. Revisit your tag list periodically — competition shifts as other models adopt the same tags.

## Tags Aren't a One-Time Setting

Because competition changes over time, a tag set that worked well a few months ago can quietly become oversaturated. If your traffic has dropped and you haven't touched your tags in a while, that's worth checking — see [Why My Traffic Dropped on Stripchat](why-traffic-dropped-on-stripchat).

## Tags Are One Piece of Discovery

Tags determine whether you're in the right pool of rooms to be found at all. Once you're there, [StripScore](how-stripscore-works) and session-level [algorithm](stripchat-algorithm) signals determine how high you sit within it.
