# Stripchat Payout: Methods, Minimums, and Schedule

Understanding how and when Stripchat actually pays out helps you plan around cash flow, not just gross earnings.

## Payment Schedule

Stripchat processes payouts automatically **every Tuesday**, covering earnings from the previous Monday–Sunday week. How fast that payout actually reaches you depends on the method you've chosen.

## Payout Methods

- **Instant e-wallets** — Paxum, CosmoPay, Yoursafe. Generally the fastest options.
- **Cryptocurrency** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether (USDT), USDC. Also among the fastest to arrive.
- **Bank transfers** — SEPA, ACH, and similar options. Reliable but slower, sometimes taking up to **10 business days**.
- **Visa** — available as a payout option in supported regions.

## Minimum Payout Thresholds

Minimums vary by method:

- Around **$100 minimum** for Paxum (with a small per-payout fee).
- Around **$100 minimum** for USDC/ETH; **$50 minimum** for BTC/USDT.
- Other methods generally process once your balance clears a **~$50** threshold, though this varies by method and region.

If your weekly balance doesn't clear the minimum for your chosen method, it typically rolls over rather than being paid out, so it's worth picking a method whose minimum matches how consistently you earn.

## Practical Payout Tips

1. **Match your method to your earnings pace.** If you're earning inconsistently or just starting out, a lower-minimum method avoids balances sitting unpaid for weeks.
2. **Budget around weekly Tuesday payouts**, not daily earnings — your actual cash flow follows the payout schedule, not your streaming schedule.
3. **Factor in transfer time**, especially for bank transfers — a 10-business-day transfer means your Tuesday payout might not be usable money for nearly two weeks.
4. **Keep payout details current.** A rejected or delayed payout due to outdated account info is an easy, avoidable cash flow problem.
