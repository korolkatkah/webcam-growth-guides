# Best Streaming Hours on Stripchat

Platform-wide traffic patterns give you a starting point — but your actual best hours depend on where your specific audience lives and when they tip. Here's how to use general patterns without over-relying on them.

## General Platform-Wide Patterns

- **North American traffic** peaks roughly **9pm–1am EST on weeknights**, with Friday and Saturday evenings drawing the most concurrent viewers.
- **Weekend afternoons (2pm–6pm EST)** also perform well for US-based audiences.
- **European audiences** peak in their local evening — catching that window (roughly your afternoon if you're several hours behind EST) can noticeably lift response and tipping from European viewers.
- Over 40% of North American models are online during US evening hours — meaning that window is also the most competitive, not just the highest-traffic.

## Session Length Matters Too

Engagement tends to peak in the first 2–3 hours of a session before tapering off. Most models find their sweet spot somewhere in the **4–6 hour** range — long enough to build momentum, not so long that energy (and viewer attention) collapses.

## Why "Best Hours" Isn't One Answer

The platform-wide peak is also the most saturated time — every model chasing the same 9pm–1am EST window is competing directly with the models with the biggest existing followings. Depending on your niche and audience, a slightly off-peak slot with less competition can outperform the "best" hour on paper.

## How to Find Your Actual Best Hours

1. Start with the general windows above as a hypothesis, not a rule.
2. Track tips and viewer counts by hour for 2–3 weeks.
3. Identify where *your* regulars actually show up — this often differs from the platform average once you have real data.
4. Keep the schedule consistent once you find it — consistency is itself a ranking factor (see [Stripchat Algorithm](stripchat-algorithm)).
