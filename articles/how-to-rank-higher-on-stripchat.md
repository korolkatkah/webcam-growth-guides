# How to Rank Higher on Stripchat

Ranking higher on Stripchat isn't controlled by one lever — it's the combined output of your StripScore, your session-level performance in the broader algorithm, and how well you've set up the basics (tags, schedule, thumbnail). This page ties those pieces together; each one has its own deep-dive if you want the full detail.

## The Three Layers of Ranking

**1. Your account-level score.** This is [StripScore](how-stripscore-works) — driven mainly by hourly earnings consistency, profile completeness, and stream reliability. See [How to Increase StripScore](how-to-increase-stripscore) for the action list.

**2. Session-level performance.** Within the broader [Stripchat algorithm](stripchat-algorithm), retention, chat activity, tip frequency, and session length all get evaluated per broadcast, not just per account.

**3. Discovery basics.** Tags, thumbnail quality, and streaming hours determine whether people ever *see* your room in the first place, before any ranking signal even applies. Covered in [Stripchat Hashtags / Tags](stripchat-hashtags-tags) and [Best Streaming Hours on Stripchat](best-streaming-hours-on-stripchat).

## A Practical Priority Order

If you're only going to fix a few things, this order tends to produce the fastest visible movement:

1. Complete your profile (photo album + video) — quick, one-time, measurable.
2. Fix your tags to a moderate-competition range instead of the biggest/most obvious ones.
3. Stabilize your schedule — same days, same hours, every week.
4. Push session length toward 90+ minutes where your content allows.
5. Actively manage chat instead of streaming passively.
6. Structure tipping around frequent small tips (tiered menu) rather than rare big ones.

## New Accounts Rank Differently

If your account is new, expect a 4–8 week calibration period before these changes fully show up in your position. Combine that with Stripchat's own new-model boost window (see [Stripchat New Model Boost](stripchat-new-model-boost)) to make the most of the head start the platform already gives you.

## Where External Promotion Fits

Ranking factors control your position *within* Stripchat's existing traffic. They don't create new traffic from outside.
