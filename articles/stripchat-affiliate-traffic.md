# Stripchat Affiliate Traffic Explained

Some of the traffic that reaches Stripchat rooms doesn't come from the platform's own discovery systems or from a model's personal promotion — it comes through Stripchat's official affiliate network. Understanding this layer matters most if you work with a studio or agency, but it's useful context for any model.

## What Stripcash Is

Stripcash is Stripchat's official affiliate/webmaster program. Affiliates (webmasters) drive outside traffic to Stripchat and earn commission based on what those referred users spend. It runs on two main models:

- **Revenue share** — typically around a **20% lifetime revshare** on referred users' spending.
- **Pay-per-lead (PPL)** — a flat fee (roughly **$3–$5**) per qualifying signup, depending on the lead type.

Affiliates also earn from referring models to the platform and from referring other affiliates into the network.

## Why This Matters to Models

If you're working with a studio or agency, some of your viewer traffic may already be arriving through affiliate channels rather than organic discovery or your own promotion — which is one reason two models with similar on-platform effort can see different growth rates. It's worth asking your studio directly whether affiliate traffic is part of your growth strategy, since it changes what "more promotion" should actually mean for you.

## Should Solo Models Get Involved With Affiliate Traffic?

Directly, usually not — Stripcash is built for webmasters and marketers running referral campaigns, not for models promoting their own room. Where it's relevant to an individual model is indirectly: through a studio or management arrangement that uses affiliate channels on your behalf.

## What Solo Models Should Focus On Instead

If you're not working through a studio, the equivalent leverage comes from:

- [Organic external traffic](stripchat-seo-external-traffic) you build yourself (social, personal page).
- [Managed traffic promotion](https://ewohub.com/stripchat-promo) designed specifically for individual models rather than affiliate-scale campaigns.
