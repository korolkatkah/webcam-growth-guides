# Lovense Settings for Stripchat: Setup Guide

Stripchat supports direct Lovense integration, letting viewers control your toy through tips without any separate third-party overlay software required.

## Basic Connection Steps

1. Open your **Model Dashboard** on Stripchat.
2. Go to **Settings → Interactive Toy → Lovense**.
3. Connect your Lovense account credentials.
4. Configure which tip amounts trigger which vibration intensities.
5. Test the connection with a small tip from a second device or account before going live.

## How Tip-Based Activation Works

When a viewer tips a specific token amount, your toy vibrates at whatever intensity you've mapped to that amount. You control the mapping — for example, a small tip might trigger a brief low-intensity pulse, while a larger tip triggers a longer high-intensity pattern. Setting clear, visible tiers (and displaying them in your bio or tip menu) helps viewers understand exactly what their tip does, which tends to increase how often they use it.

## Full Toy Control

Stripchat also offers a **Full Toy Control** feature, available in both public and private shows. When enabled, viewers can directly choose a vibration level (low, medium, high, and other custom options) rather than relying only on preset tip-triggered patterns. This gives viewers a stronger sense of direct interaction, which is one of the reasons interactive-toy rooms tend to see meaningfully higher tip volume than rooms without toy integration.

## Practical Tips

- Test intensity levels on yourself before a live session — what feels right in settings doesn't always match what happens in practice.
- Keep your tip-to-intensity mapping visible in your bio and repeat it in chat periodically, similar to how you'd manage a general tip menu.
- Have a backup plan (manual control) in case the connection drops mid-show — toy disconnects during an active show are a common source of dead air.
- Combine toy interaction with goals, since a group-funded escalation often outperforms individual random tips.
