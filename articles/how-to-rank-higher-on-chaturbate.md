# How to Rank Higher on Chaturbate

Ranking higher on Chaturbate comes down to a handful of interlocking factors: how often people click on you, how long they stay, how active your room is, and how consistent your schedule is. This page ties those pieces together — each has its own deep-dive if you want the full detail.

## The Layers of Ranking

**1. Discovery basics.** Your thumbnail, title, and hashtags determine whether people ever see and click on your room in the first place. See [Chaturbate Hashtags](chaturbate-hashtags) and [How to Get on Chaturbate Front Page](how-to-get-on-chaturbate-front-page).

**2. Session performance.** Once someone clicks in, [Chaturbate's algorithm](chaturbate-algorithm) evaluates retention, chat activity, and tipping — this is where a strong opening and active room management pay off.

**3. Consistency signals.** A fixed daily schedule and a growing, returning follower base tell the algorithm your room is worth promoting repeatedly, not just once.

**4. New-account mechanics.** If you're new, Chaturbate's own [new model tag](chaturbate-new-model-tag) gives you a temporary visibility boost — worth understanding and using deliberately rather than wasting.

## A Practical Priority Order

1. Fix your thumbnail and title first — this determines whether anyone clicks at all.
2. Rotate hashtags on a schedule rather than leaving the same ones indefinitely.
3. Design your first few minutes on stream to hook viewers immediately.
4. Lock in a consistent daily streaming schedule.
5. Actively manage chat instead of streaming passively — engagement outranks passive viewer count.
6. Use referral/affiliate signups as a secondary growth lever if you're set up for it.

## Where External Promotion Fits

These factors control your position within Chaturbate's existing traffic pool — they don't create new traffic from outside.
