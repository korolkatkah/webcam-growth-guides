# Stripchat Private Show Tips: Pricing and Strategy

Private shows are one of the highest per-minute revenue streams available on Stripchat, but pricing and setup decisions have a big effect on how often viewers actually convert into private sessions.

## Typical Pricing

- **Private shows** are commonly priced around **8–12 tokens per minute**, with the specific rate set by the model. Most models land near 8 tokens/minute as a baseline.
- **Spy shows** let another viewer watch an ongoing private show without participating, typically for **4–60 tokens per minute** (model-set). Spying viewers can't chat or make requests — it's a lower-cost, lower-engagement option layered on top of your regular private rate.
- Token value to the model is roughly **$0.05 per token** (100 tokens ≈ $5), so per-minute private rates translate directly into an hourly-equivalent you can compare against your public-room tip average.

## Getting Viewers Into Private Shows

1. **Tease, don't hide.** Give public-room viewers enough to know what they're paying for — mystery works less well than a clear sense of what a private show includes.
2. **Set a price you'll actually honor consistently.** Constantly changing your per-minute rate confuses regulars and undermines trust.
3. **Use spy shows as a bridge.** A viewer unsure about committing to a full private session may convert through the lower-cost spy option first, then upgrade once they're invested.
4. **Keep private show availability visible** in your bio and mentioned periodically in public chat, the same way you'd repeat a tip menu.

## Private Shows vs. Public Tipping

Public-room income depends on volume and a well-structured tip menu (see [How to Earn More Tokens on Stripchat](how-to-earn-more-tokens-on-stripchat)). Private shows convert a smaller number of highly interested viewers into guaranteed per-minute income — the two aren't competing strategies, they're complementary revenue streams that work best together.
