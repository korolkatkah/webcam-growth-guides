# Chaturbate Room Ranking: Action Checklist

If you already understand [how Chaturbate's algorithm works](chaturbate-algorithm), this is the checklist version — concrete actions in rough priority order for moving your room ranking up.

## 1. Fix Click-Through First

Since CTR (how often your thumbnail gets clicked when shown) is a core ranking input, a stronger preview image and sharper title is usually the single highest-leverage change available, regardless of what else you fix.

## 2. Design for Retention, Not Just Clicks

Getting a click only matters if the viewer stays. Build your opening minutes to hook attention immediately — an engaging first impression converts clicks into the retention signal the algorithm rewards.

## 3. Rotate Hashtags Deliberately

Stale tags lose effectiveness as competition shifts. Set a recurring reminder to review and refresh your tag set rather than leaving it static for months. See [Chaturbate Hashtags](chaturbate-hashtags).

## 4. Lock In a Consistent Schedule

Streaming at the same time daily builds the "sticky room" signal the algorithm favors, and it's one of the few ranking factors that's entirely within your control regardless of luck or audience size.

## 5. Actively Manage the Room

Chat, react, and prompt tips rather than streaming passively. Room activity — chatting, tipping, following, interacting — is weighed independently of raw viewer count.

## 6. Use Referral Signups as a Secondary Lever

Bringing in new registered users through your Chaturbate affiliate link adds another concrete ranking input on top of engagement metrics. See [Chaturbate Affiliate Traffic](chaturbate-affiliate-traffic).

## 7. Use Your New Model Status If You Have It

If you're new, the platform's own [new model tag](chaturbate-new-model-tag) gives you a temporary head start — treat it as a limited-time opportunity, not a permanent advantage.

## Ranking Controls Position Within Existing Traffic

Everything above improves where you sit in Chaturbate's own traffic pool — it doesn't add new viewers from outside. [EWOhub's model promotion](https://ewohub.com/model-promotion) extends growth by adding external traffic on top of a solid ranking foundation.
