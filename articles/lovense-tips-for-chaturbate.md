# Lovense Tips for Chaturbate

Chaturbate supports Lovense through a browser extension that activates automatically once you're broadcasting, adding a "Lovense Control" option directly to your tip menu.

## How the Connection Works

1. Install the Lovense browser extension and pair it with the Lovense Connect app on your computer.
2. Log into your Chaturbate broadcaster account and start your stream — the extension activates automatically.
3. Enable **Lovense Control** in your tip menu settings.
4. Test the connection before going live to confirm tip-to-vibration mapping is working.

The flow from that point is: a viewer tips → the extension detects the event → it signals the Lovense Connect app → Connect sends a Bluetooth command to your toy → the toy responds, typically in under half a second.

## Setting Tip-to-Intensity Mapping

You can configure as many tip levels as you like — a low tip might trigger a brief, gentle pulse, while a premium tip triggers a longer, stronger pattern. Clear, visible tiers (posted in your bio or tip menu) help viewers understand exactly what their tip does, which tends to increase how often they use it.

## Viewer-Controlled Mode

Some setups support direct viewer control of intensity in real time as a paid feature, rather than only preset tip-triggered patterns. This gives viewers a stronger sense of direct interaction, which is part of why interactive-toy rooms often see meaningfully higher tip volume than rooms without it.

## Practical Tips

- Test intensity levels on yourself before a live session — settings that look right on paper don't always feel right in practice.
- Add a toy tag (like #lovense) to your hashtags so viewers specifically looking for interactive shows can find you — see [Chaturbate Hashtags](chaturbate-hashtags).
- Keep your tip-to-intensity mapping visible and repeat it in chat periodically, the same way you'd manage a general tip menu.
- Have a manual fallback plan in case the Bluetooth connection drops mid-show.
