# How to Get on the Chaturbate Front Page

The Chaturbate front page (the "featured" list) is chosen entirely by algorithm, not human curation — which means it responds to specific, identifiable signals rather than luck.

## What the Algorithm Actually Weighs Here

- **Token-holding viewers in your room.** What matters is how many users with tokens are currently watching — not whether any single viewer has 2 tokens or 2 million.
- **Click-through rate.** How often your thumbnail gets clicked when it's shown determines a large part of your placement — a strong preview image and title matter as much as your actual show.
- **Referral signups.** Chaturbate favors broadcasters who bring in new registered users through their own affiliate link — several signups combined with solid views and tips can move you up quickly. See [Chaturbate Affiliate Traffic](chaturbate-affiliate-traffic).
- **Hashtags.** Since Chaturbate has no official categories, the tags in your room status/title double as your primary discovery mechanism — the most popular tags surface directly on the main page. See [Chaturbate Hashtags](chaturbate-hashtags).
- **Consistent presence.** A detailed bio with your schedule helps members plan around your hours, and habitual streaming times build the kind of returning traffic the algorithm rewards.

## A Practical Checklist

1. Rebuild your thumbnail and title around what actually earns clicks, not just what looks nice to you.
2. Pick hashtags deliberately and rotate them — stale tags lose their pull as competition shifts.
3. Fill out a complete, specific bio including your schedule.
4. If you're comfortable with it, actively drive referral signups through your affiliate link.
5. Stream at consistent times so returning viewers (and the algorithm) can rely on your schedule.

## This Hasn't Changed Much Over Time

The core logic behind front-page placement has stayed largely consistent since the platform launched — it isn't chasing new metrics each year, it's rewarding the same fundamentals: genuine engagement and referral growth.
