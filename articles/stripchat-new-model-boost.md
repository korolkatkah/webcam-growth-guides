# Stripchat New Model Boost: What It Is and How to Use It

New Stripchat models get a temporary ranking advantage designed to give fresh accounts a fair chance to be seen against established performers. How you use that window has an outsized effect on everything that follows.

## How the Boost Works

Historically, the boost period runs about **15 days** from your first stream, giving new rooms elevated placement in the model list regardless of their still-developing StripScore. More recent platform updates have extended new-model promotion closer to a **full month** in some cases, giving newcomers even more runway.

## Realistic Early Earnings

Based on typical new-model patterns: the first few days often bring in around **$5–$10**, scaling to roughly **$50/day** after the first month of consistent effort, with models who stick with it crossing **$100/day** somewhere in the 2–3 month range. These are typical patterns, not guarantees — but they're useful for setting realistic expectations instead of quitting during a slow first week.

## How to Make the Most of the Boost Window

1. **Be available as much as realistically possible** during the boost period — the visibility is temporary, and the audience you capture during it can become your regulars afterward.
2. **Prioritize chat engagement over passive streaming.** The boost gets you seen; engagement is what converts that visibility into tips and returning viewers.
3. **Complete your profile before your first stream**, not after — photo album and video both feed into StripScore from day one.
4. **Don't change your whole approach if the first few days are slow.** Early earnings in the $5–$10/day range are typical, not a sign to abandon the strategy.
5. **Transition deliberately.** As the boost window ends, your position starts depending more on [StripScore](how-stripscore-works) fundamentals — have your schedule, tags, and profile fully dialed in before that happens, not after.

## After the Boost Ends

The new-account calibration period (roughly 4–8 weeks) overlaps with and extends past the boost window. Losing some visibility as the boost fades is normal and doesn't necessarily mean something is wrong — see [Why My Traffic Dropped on Stripchat](why-traffic-dropped-on-stripchat) if the drop feels larger than expected.
