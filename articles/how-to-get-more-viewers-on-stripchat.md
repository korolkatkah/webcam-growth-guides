# How to Get More Viewers on Stripchat

Most models trying to get more viewers on Stripchat focus only on what happens inside the room. In practice, the biggest wins usually come from a handful of fixable issues outside the show itself.

## The Most Common Viewer Killers

- **Wrong streaming time.** Streaming outside your audience's active hours can cut your effective reach dramatically — sometimes by an order of magnitude. See [Best Streaming Hours on Stripchat](best-streaming-hours-on-stripchat) for specific windows.
- **Generic tags.** Broad, high-competition tags bury your room. Moderate-competition tags (roughly 500–2,000 rooms using them) tend to convert better than the biggest, most crowded ones. More in [Stripchat Hashtags / Tags](stripchat-hashtags-tags).
- **Weak preview thumbnail.** Poor lighting, an awkward angle, or a flat expression in your live preview quietly kills click-through before anyone even sees your show.
- **No presence outside the platform.** Relying only on Stripchat's internal discovery caps your ceiling. Even a modest social following (a few hundred engaged followers) measurably helps funnel viewers in.
- **Inconsistent schedule.** Irregular streaming hurts both your standing with the algorithm and your ability to build a returning audience who knows when to show up.
- **New-account calibration.** New rooms typically need 4–8 weeks before Stripchat's systems fully "learn" the account. Many models quit right before this period pays off.

## What Actually Moves the Needle

1. Fix your streaming schedule to match real audience activity, not convenience.
2. Pick 3–5 tags in the moderate-competition range instead of the two or three biggest ones.
3. Re-shoot your thumbnail with better lighting — this alone often lifts click-through noticeably.
4. Post consistently on one outside platform (even just one) to build a channel that isn't dependent on Stripchat's algorithm.
5. Keep the room active with chat and small tip prompts — engagement is itself a ranking signal, covered in [How StripScore Works](how-stripscore-works).
