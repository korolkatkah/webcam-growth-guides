# Chaturbate Promotion: Every Type of Traffic Strategy Explained

"Chaturbate promotion" gets used to describe several genuinely different strategies. Knowing which one you're actually doing — or missing — makes it much easier to diagnose slow growth.

## 1. Internal (On-Platform) Promotion

Everything Chaturbate's own systems control: the [ranking algorithm](chaturbate-algorithm), your [new model tag](chaturbate-new-model-tag) window, hashtag discovery, and front-page placement. Free, but bounded by the platform's existing traffic.

## 2. Organic External Promotion

Building an audience on social platforms, forums, or a personal page, then funneling that audience into your room. Slower to build but not rented — the audience is yours. See [Chaturbate External Traffic](chaturbate-external-traffic) and [How to Get More Followers on Chaturbate](how-to-get-more-followers-on-chaturbate).

## 3. Managed Traffic Promotion

Dedicated services that drive additional real viewers into your room from outside the platform, layered on top of your existing ranking and content. [EWOhub's model promotion](https://ewohub.com/model-promotion) is built to compound with — not replace — your organic and internal standing.

## 4. Affiliate-Driven Traffic

Traffic sent by webmasters and affiliates through Chaturbate's own affiliate network, which also pays broadcasters directly for referring new models. More in [Chaturbate Affiliate Traffic](chaturbate-affiliate-traffic).

## Which One Should You Focus On?

- **New account, no following:** start with internal basics — thumbnail, hashtags, schedule — and make full use of your new model tag window.
- **Established room, plateaued:** organic external promotion usually unlocks the next tier of growth.
- **Need faster results now:** managed promotion adds volume without waiting for organic channels to mature.
- **Comfortable with affiliate marketing:** referral signups are a legitimate secondary lever, and Chaturbate's program is open to broadcasters directly, not just webmasters.

Most models who grow steadily use at least two of these four at once rather than betting on a single channel. EWOhub's [studio traffic page](https://ewohub.com/studio-traffic) covers how this looks for studios managing multiple models rather than a single room.
