# How to Earn More Tokens on Stripchat

Earning more on Stripchat isn't primarily about attracting more viewers — it's about converting the viewers you already have. Platform data consistently shows that a room with 50 engaged, tipping viewers earns more than one with 500 silent lurkers.

## Understand the Split

Stripchat pays models a percentage of the gross token revenue spent in their room — typically in the **20–50% range**, with most active models landing around **35–45%**. Studio-affiliated models generally earn a different share (30–50% of what the studio collects), since the studio takes a cut for management and infrastructure.

## Where the Tokens Actually Come From

- **Public room tips** — the base layer; best driven by a structured tip menu (small/mid/premium tiers) rather than hoping for occasional large tips.
- **Private shows** — typically priced around **8–12 tokens per minute**, set by the model.
- **Spy shows** — viewers can pay a smaller per-minute rate (roughly **4–60 tokens**, model-set) to watch an ongoing private show without participating. See [Stripchat Private Show Tips](stripchat-private-show-tips).
- **Fan clubs and subscriptions** — recurring revenue that doesn't depend on being live.
- **Premium content sales** — clips, photo sets, and similar one-time purchases.

## What Actually Increases Token Volume

1. **Streaming during your real peak hours** — peak-hour sessions can bring in 3–5x more per hour than off-peak ones. See [Best Streaming Hours on Stripchat](best-streaming-hours-on-stripchat).
2. **Maintaining 20–60 regular tippers.** High earners consistently point to a stable core of regulars rather than constant new-viewer turnover as their main income driver.
3. **Diversifying beyond live tips** — private shows, clips, and fan clubs reduce how dependent your income is on any single live session.
4. **Building external traffic.** Off-platform promotion doesn't just add viewers — it specifically adds people who already have context on you, which converts to tokens faster than cold platform traffic.
5. **Tenure.** Models who stick with a platform for 3–6 months and let their regular audience compound consistently outearn otherwise-identical newer accounts.
